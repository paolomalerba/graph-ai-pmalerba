About Me

I am a PhD candidate at CRIMEDIM (Research Center for Disaster Medicine, Humanitarian Aid, and Global Health) with a medical background and a specialization in Internal Medicine. My research focuses on the destructive impact of armed conflicts on health systems and possible solutions to ensure continuity of care for conflict-affected populations. Passionate about social justice, I advocate for health as a fundamental human right, particularly for marginalized populations.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week live online certification focused on no‑code AI tools for task automation and research support. Key outcomes include:

  • Automate document, spreadsheet, and data workflows
  • Organize knowledge and accelerate literature reviews
  • Build custom chatbots and AI presentations
  • Complete a hands‑on capstone project
